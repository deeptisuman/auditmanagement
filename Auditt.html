Audit Pointers
Call Audit Pointers:
  • Clarity of communication and tone used by the agent.
  • Proper issue identification and probing techniques.
  • Accuracy and relevance of information provided.
  • Adherence to company policies and protocols.
  • Resolution provided during the call or escalation handled appropriately.
Ticket Audit Pointers:
  • Completeness of ticket details and categorization.
  • Timeliness and accuracy of responses.
  • Proper documentation of interactions linked to the ticket.
  • Resolution status and adherence to SLAs.
  • Escalation and reassignment processes followed correctly.
